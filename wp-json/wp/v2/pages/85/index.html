Real Spaces<\/h1>\n

At our ICS Forth Building in Heraklion, Crete and the Etoile Building of Telecom Sudparis, we have established laboratory facilities to test and deploy devices, algorithms, and IoT applications. Our lab spaces are designed to be flexible and adaptable to the changing needs of our research.<\/p>\n <\/div>\n <\/section>\n

Virtual Spaces<\/h1>\n

We also have a virtual space that provides a simulated environments. 
One of this is a virtual representation of an office based on the floor plan of the TV series \"The Office\". These kinds of virtual spaces allow us to test IoT applications in simulated scenarios and gain valuable insights for our research and development.\n <\/p>\n <\/div>\n <\/section>\n \n \n \n \n<\/div>","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
