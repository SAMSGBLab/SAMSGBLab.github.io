The Lab

The SATRAI Living Lab is dedicated to conducting cutting-edge research in the field of IoT, led by Assoc. Professor Georgios Bouloukakis. It is located at the Etoile building of the Télécom SudParis Evry Campus.

Our Research

Our research focuses on five main areas of reseach: IoT, Smart Communities & Spaces, Middleware, AI and Digital Twins.

Joining us

We are always interested in working with new people. We hold weekly research meetings during the academic year that are open to visitors. You are welcome to join us to find out what we are working on. Please contact us if you are in government or industry and interested in collaborative research, are a prospective graduate student interested in applying for our graduate programs, or are currently a Télécom SudParis or Computer Science Department undergraduate student who would like to get involved with research.
